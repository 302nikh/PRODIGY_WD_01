Kalsubai Peak Trek

KALSUBAI PEAK, the highest point in Maharashtra at 1,646 meters, offers an exhilarating trekking experience. Starting from Bari village, the trek features lush forests, rugged paths, and steel ladders aiding the climb. At the summit, enjoy panoramic views of the Sahyadri range and visit the small temple dedicated to the local deity, Kalsubai. Ideal for monsoon and winter treks, Kalsubai promises a blend of adventure and natural beauty.

Kalavanti Durg Trek

KALAVANTI DURG , a pinnacle fort near Panvel in Maharashtra, is renowned for its thrilling trek and stunning views. The trek starts from Thakurwadi village, leading through lush greenery and steep, rocky terrain. The highlight is the final ascent, involving rock-cut steps that offer a vertical climb. At the top, trekkers are rewarded with breathtaking vistas of the Sahyadri range and nearby Prabalgad fort. Best undertaken in the monsoon or winter, this trek is perfect for adventure enthusiasts seeking a unique and challenging experience.
